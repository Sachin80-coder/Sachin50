About FixFinder

Connecting homeowners with trusted professionals for all their repair and maintenance needs

Our Story

FixFinder was born out of a simple frustration: finding reliable, trustworthy service providers for home repairs shouldn't be a stressful experience. Too often, homeowners struggle to find qualified professionals, worry about fair pricing, and question the quality of work.

Founded in 2024, we set out to create a platform that bridges the gap between homeowners needing repairs and skilled professionals looking to grow their businesses. We believe that every home deserves quality care, and every skilled worker deserves fair opportunities.

Today, FixFinder serves thousands of customers across India, facilitating connections that result in fixed homes and thriving service businesses.
