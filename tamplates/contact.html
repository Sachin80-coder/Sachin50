Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I find service providers in my area?

Simply enter your location and service type in our search. We'll show you verified professionals nearby with reviews and ratings.

Are all service providers verified?

Yes, we conduct background checks and verify credentials for all service providers before they can join our platform.

How much does it cost to use FixFinder?

It's completely free for customers to use FixFinder. You only pay the service provider directly for their work.

What if I'm not satisfied with the service?

We have a customer satisfaction guarantee. Contact our support team and we'll work with you and the provider to resolve any issues.

How can I become a service provider?

Click "Become a Provider" and complete our registration process. We'll verify your credentials and help you get started.

Is there customer support available?

Yes, our customer support team is available during business hours via phone, email, and live chat to help with any questions.
